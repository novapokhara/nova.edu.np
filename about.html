Home 🢒 About Nova

NOVA is a highly professional and experienced computer education centre based in mountain-nestled Pokhara. Comprising of faculty considered the best in the industry. The courses at NOVA are affordable with flexible payment schemes. Special discounts are also available for ex-students and to those who upgrade to higher course.

A unique 3 models, well-planned, comprehensive approach integrated Diploma in Advance Computing Studies (IDACS-IT Professionals) or Computer Services Technician (CST-Hardware) courses taking courses you from the basic to intermediates and finally to the advanced levels. To provide hands on experience, all the students have to undergo daily practical sessions at our well-equipped labs. Our focuses approach and committed faculty members who are constantly upgrading the courses to make them more industrious and specific towards the field. NOVA has assisted many students over the past 17 years of its operations in Nepal towards stabilizing their career home and abroad, we support and have supported our clients. We personally assist all the abroad-going students for choosing their desired study, course/requirements, documentation, administration and visa preparation.
